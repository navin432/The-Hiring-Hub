Laptop/Desktop Computer Details

Laptop/Desktop Computer

Models: Dell XPS 15 (Laptop) / HP Omen 30L (Desktop)

Processor:

Laptop: Intel Core i7-13700H or AMD Ryzen 7 6800H

Desktop: AMD Ryzen 9 7900X or Intel Core i9-12900K

RAM:

Laptop: 16GB DDR5 (expandable to 32GB)

Desktop: 32GB DDR5 (expandable to 64GB)

Storage:

Laptop: 512GB or 1TB NVMe SSD

Desktop: 1TB NVMe SSD (optional 2TB HDD)

Graphics:

Laptop: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4050 or 4060

Desktop: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 or AMD Radeon RX 6800

Display Size:

Laptop: 15.6 inches (FHD or 4K)

Desktop: Typically paired with a 27-inch monitor.
