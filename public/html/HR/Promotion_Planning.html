Promotion Criteria and Policies

To be eligible for a promotion, employees must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum of 2 years in the current position.
  • Consistently meets or exceeds performance targets.
  • Completed all required training and certifications.
  • Positive feedback from direct managers and peers.
